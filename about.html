About Holidae

Holidae is a simple holiday deals website built for people who browse on their phone and book later on a laptop. You get clear package details, fast loading pages, and a layout that stays easy to read on any screen.

What Holidae does

Hand-picked packages

You see a small set of deals with the key information upfront. Nights, board basis, transfers, and price. No endless scrolling.

Mobile-first design

Most people browse on mobile. Holidae keeps buttons large, text readable, and images responsive so you can compare deals quickly.

Simple next steps

Each deal has a clear call to action. View details, check availability, then move toward booking when you are ready.

Transparent pricing

Prices are shown as “from” because they can change by date and availability. You always see what is included before you enquire.

What you can expect

  • Fast pages with a clean layout
  • Easy navigation for phone and desktop
  • Clear package summaries and simple detail pages
  • Accessible structure with headings, labels, and alt text

Why Holidae was made

Holiday websites can feel busy. Too many pop-ups, too many filters, and too much text makes it hard to decide. Holidae focuses on a small number of strong deals and presents them in a calm way.

You might browse on a commute and come back later to book. Holidae is built around that habit, so your experience stays consistent between devices.
